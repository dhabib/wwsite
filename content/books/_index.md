## Latent Vector

![Latent Vector](/images/latent-vector.jpg)

**AI and the unlocking of a $5 trillion filing cabinet.**

An examination of how converging AI technologies reshape business coordination. Applies transaction cost economics to explore corporate disruption and critiques the Information-Commercial Complex fighting to preserve profitable existing structures.

Print: [Barnes & Noble](https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/latent-vector-david-habib/1148189965?ean=9798218785529) · Ebook: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Latent-Vector-David-Habib-ebook/dp/B0FPPSB9LZ?ref_=ast_author_dp&th=1&psc=1)

## About 5 Years

![About 5 Years](/images/about-5-years.jpg)

**How online content businesses will navigate the next half-decade of disruption.**

Analysis of industry transformation as discovery shifts from pull-based to algorithm-driven push-based models. Addresses Google's evolution, short-form video dominance, and privacy-driven advertising changes affecting content creators.

Print & Ebook: [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/About-Years-Businesses-Half-Decade-Disruption/dp/B0F8W1JP7D/)
