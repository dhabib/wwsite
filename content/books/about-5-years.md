## How online content businesses will navigate the next half-decade of disruption

Analysis of industry transformation as discovery shifts from pull-based (active audience seeking) to algorithm-driven push-based models. Addresses Google's evolution, short-form video dominance, and privacy-driven advertising changes affecting content creators. Examines the impacts of search engine evolution and the transition from traditional content discovery methods.
