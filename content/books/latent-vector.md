## AI and the unlocking of a $5 trillion filing cabinet

An examination of how converging AI technologies reshape business coordination. The work applies transaction cost economics to explore corporate disruption and critiques the Information-Commercial Complex fighting to preserve profitable existing structures. Discusses how the convergence of all information into AI-readable formats represents significant economic disruption.
