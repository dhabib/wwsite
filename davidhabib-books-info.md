## Book 2: Latent Vector

### Basic Information
- **Title:** Latent Vector
- **Subtitle:** AI and the unlocking of a $5 trillion filing cabinet
- **Genre:** Nonfiction / Business / Technology
- **ISBN (Print):** 9798218785529

### Description
An examination of how converging AI technologies reshape business coordination. The work applies transaction cost economics to explore corporate disruption and critiques the Information-Commercial Complex fighting to preserve profitable existing structures. Discusses how the convergence of all information into AI-readable formats represents significant economic disruption.

## Book 3: About 5 Years

### Basic Information
- **Title:** About 5 Years
- **Subtitle:** How online content businesses will navigate the next half-decade of disruption
- **Genre:** Nonfiction / Business / Digital Media

### Description
Analysis of industry transformation as discovery shifts from pull-based (active audience seeking) to algorithm-driven push-based models. Addresses Google's evolution, short-form video dominance, and privacy-driven advertising changes affecting content creators. Examines the impacts of search engine evolution and the transition from traditional content discovery methods.
